Decoration

Not only the preparation of the cake is important, but what makes a very professional dessert look is its decoration at all. We have floral decorations that fit all kinds of special occasions, from small flowers to flowers of larger sizes even in jellies. These decorations are very simple since they only need our special touch. 3D decorations are usually seen for their level of difficulties, but we have tips that you could not believe if it were not on our page.

Some of them are made with wafers or light projector on the pastel and the drawings or effects are made more accurately to the design or photograph required. Geometric decoration focuses on figures that the cake can adapt using different molds than the traditional one that is used regularly. You can learn to make figures of hearts, triangles, squares, etc. Even the right size for the portions of people who will consume the cake, are small details that make a big difference.

Finally naked decoration, this type of design rose in popularity lately, since it can be decorated in various ways, with flowers, fondant, chocolate, cake cream or even in cookies and desserts. In fact, you can add all the styles of decoration in this last naked decoration.
